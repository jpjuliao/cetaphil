Why did Galderma discontinue the Anti-Bacterial Bar?

  • In 2016 the FDA issued a ruling that triclosan and triclocarbon would no longer be permitted to be used in personal care products by the end of 2017
  • The FDA ruled there was not enough evidence to support triclosan and triclocarbon were more effective than just regular soap and water at removing surface bacteria
  • Triclosan and triclocarbon are still being utilized in hospital disinfecting but will no longer be used in consumer personal care products
  • The FDA decision was based solely on a lack of evidence of triclosan and triclocarbon efficacy
